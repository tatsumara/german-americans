Marlene Dietrich, who was a German-American actress and singer, was born in Berlin on 27th December 1901 and died in Paris on 6th May 1992.

She was the Hollywood and style icon of the 20th century. Her signature traits were her long legs, her deep smoky-erotic voice and her iconic overalls.

Marlene began her career with theater and silent movies in Berlin. After that she got the leading role in the movie "The blue Angel" in 1930, which won her international fame.
