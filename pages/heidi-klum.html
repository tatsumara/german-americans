Heidi Klum

Heidi Klum (legal name: Heidi Kaulitz after recent marriage) was born on June 1st 1973 in Bergisch Gladbach, Germany and became an American citizen in 2008 while still maintaining her German citizenship. She is one of the world's best known models/tv-hosts. After winning "Model92", a German casting show, in 1992, she moved to America to pursue a career as a professional model.

She accomplished her goal and went on to model for numerous big brands, such as Victoria's Secret, where she became the first German "Angel", and posing for magazine covers such as Vogue or Sports Illustrated.

In the past couple of years, she has basically retired from modeling and has since ventured into the tv-business as a tv-host of the American tv-show "Project Runway", which she has also won an Emmy for, and a co-host/-producer of the German tv-show Germanys Next Topmodel.
