Sandra Bullock

Sandra Bullock was born on the 26 of July in 1964 in Airlington, Virgina. At the age of 18 she had both American and German citizenship.

She trained as an actress at East California University. Studies were harder than she thought, so she dropped out. She moved to Los Angeles und got her first role in the movie Hangman.

Her first appearance as the main role was in the film Demolition Man. Her appearance in the film Speed brought the breakthrough of her career.

She wrote a book about a boxer herself, which was released as a movie in 2004. She also received a series of awards, for example “best leading actress” in the movies Gravity and Speed. In the year 2005, she married Jesse James.

She also founded her own production company called Fortis Films and opened a gourmet restaurant. Today, she is one of the best-payed actresses in Hollywood. Currently she lives in Austin, Texas.
