Steinway & Sons

Steinway & Sons, also known as Steinway, is a German-American high quality piano company. It was founded by the German piano builder Heinrich Engelhard Steinweg (Henry E. Steinway) in Manhattan, New York City, in 1853. The employees at Steinway were mostly German immigrants.

The factory in Queens supplied the Americas, and the factory in Hamburg supplied the rest of the world. 2020 Steinway amassed over 125 patents, completely dominating the piano world. The price for a Steinway piano ranges from 17.000 to 55.000 euros, some rare models may also cost over 2.5 million euros.
